Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1948

Consists of an interactive curriculum that keep both teachers and students on their toes. They have many sports for the students to play in like basketball and volleyball.



School Library

Was established in 1980

It is a fun place where students can read books and interact with their peers.



School Auditorium

Was established in 1982

It allows the principal to make important anouncements to all the students. It is also used as a place for special guests to come visit and entertain the children.

School Achievements

Interschool Debate on Environment
Won first place in debate on environmental pollution

Our school won first place in the debate whether laws for environmental pollution should be tighter. Our students battled hard against other students and we came on top. All this was because of our amazing debate program.

Interschool Football Competition
Won second place in football competition

Our school won second place in the annual Interschool Football Competion. Our team struggled but we came into the finals and placed second place. This was thanks to our amazing football coaches.

Interschool Science Project Competition
Won first place for Interschool Science Competition

Our school won second place in the annual Interschool Science Project Competition. Our students racked their minds over these projects that were very impressive. Our teachers helped teach the children what they would need to know to get their projects going so thanks to them.
